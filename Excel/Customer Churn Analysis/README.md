# Customer Churn Analysis: Telecom Case Study

### **Business Context**
This project analyzes a churn dataset from a fictitious Telecom provider, Databel. The objective was to identify the primary drivers behind customer attrition and provide data-driven recommendations to improve retention.

#### **Customer Demographics**
* **Age Groups & Competition:**  Senior customers (age 78 - 88) & those lured by competitors with better offer show the highest churn rate at 43.8 % & 37.64 % respectively.

#### **Plan Usage**
* **Low Consumption:** Customers consuming less than **5GB** of data have the highest churn rate at **34.71%**, suggesting the unlimited plan may not be the right fit for low-usage users.

#### **International & Regional Trends**
* **International Plan Inactivity:** Customers who pay for an international plan have a staggering churn rate of **75**.
* **Geographic Hotspot:** A significant portion of these high-churn international plan holders are located in **California (CA)**.

---

### **Recommended Actions**
1.  **Senior Advocacy Initiative:** Reduce the churn rate by launching a dedicated "Priority Support" line and simplifying billing statements to improve accessibility and brand loyalty among older demographics.
2. **Plan Optimization:** Review the value proposition of the "Unlimited Plan" for low-data users (<5GB). Transitioning these users to a more cost-effective, tiered plan may increase loyalty.
3. **International Plan Audit:** Proactively reach out to customers in California with international plans to offer a plan review or a usage credit before they churn.
