## Analyzing Customer Churn in Excel
- As a consultant your task is to analyze why customers are churning in other words leaving the company.
- You will be using a fictitious churn dataset from a Telecom provider called Databel. 
- This Databel dataset consists of different columns and has one row per customer.

*** Insights discovered 
- Demographics: Seniors is found to have the highest churn rate at 38.22%.
- Age Groups: 79-88 makes up the lowest number of customers but the highest churn rate at 43.80%.
- Unlimited Plan: Those with an unlimited plan who consume less than 5 GB of data records the highest churn rate at 34.69%.
- International Calls: Customers with CA state code on international plan records the highest churn rate at 75.00%.
- Contract Type: Account length 1-12 month to month contract records the highest chrun rate at 81.68%.

Recommended Actions
- Demographics: Launch a dedicated loyalty initiative or simplified support line to address the 38.22% churn rate among seniors.
- Age Groups: Perform direct outreach to the 79-88 age bracket to identify service gaps causing their 43.80% churn rate.
- Unlimited Plan: Move low-usage customers (under 5 GB) to smaller data plans to reduce their 34.69% churn rate through better price-value alignment.
- International Calls: Audit California-specific service quality and international pricing to resolve the 75.00% churn rate in that region.
- Contract Type: Provide early-tenure incentives to convert month-to-month users into long-term contracts to stabilize the 81.68% churn risk.
