# Google Analytics USA 2021: Dashboard Analysis

**Business Context**
This project involves a comprehensive analysis of Google Analytics (GA4) data for the United States in 2021. The goal was to identify geographic revenue distribution, user acquisition patterns, and engagement metrics to provide actionable business recommendations.

### **Insights Discovered**

**Revenue Distribution**
Most revenue is concentrated in Calfornia, Washington & New York, leaving the middle of the country mostly untapped.

**Order Volume**
California is the powerhouse. It recorded 9,230 transactions, nearly 5x more than the next closest state, New York.

**User Acquisition**
Over 54% of all users come from just one state—California—showing a high dependency on a single geographic market.

**User Engagement**
California and Colorado have the most engaged users (7–8 pages per visit), while users in states like Alabama only view 4 pages.

---

### **Recommended Actions**

1. **Protect the Core (CA)**
Implement loyalty rewards for California users, as they are the primary backbone of the business.

2. **Grow Tier 2 States**
Increase marketing spend in New York, Texas, and Washington to reduce the heavy reliance on a single state.

3. **Fix Engagement Gaps**
Investigate why users in the South (e.g., Alabama) leave the site quickly—check for shipping costs or regional product relevance.
